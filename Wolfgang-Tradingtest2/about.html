About Wolfgang Trading

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted wholesale supplier

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Wolfgang Trading began as a small family business with a passion for bringing farm-fresh produce to local markets. Today, we've grown into a leading wholesale supplier serving restaurants, hotels, and retail stores across the region.

Our success comes from our commitment to quality, freshness, and building lasting relationships with both farmers and customers.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between farms and markets by delivering the freshest, highest quality produce, dry fruits, and staples while maintaining fair prices and sustainable practices.
