Our Quality Commitment

Rigorous standards from farm to delivery ensure you receive only the best

Quality You Can Trust

At Wolfgang Trading, we understand that quality is not just a process, but a promise to our customers. Our multi-stage quality assurance program ensures that every product meets our high standards before it reaches you.

Farm Selection

We partner only with certified farms that meet our sustainability and ethical standards.

Visual Inspection

Every batch undergoes thorough visual checks for color, size, and freshness.

Temperature Control

Maintaining optimal temperatures throughout storage and transit preserves freshness.

Our Quality Process

1

Farm Verification

We conduct regular audits of our partner farms to ensure they meet our agricultural and labor standards.

2

Harvest Monitoring

Our field agents ensure produce is harvested at peak ripeness and handled with care to prevent bruising.

3

Packhouse Inspection

Trained inspectors check for size, color, and defects before packaging.

4

Warehouse Checks

Temperature-controlled storage with regular quality checks during warehousing.

5

Final Delivery Check

Products undergo a final inspection before being loaded onto our refrigerated trucks.
